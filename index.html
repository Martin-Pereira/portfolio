Brain

Left Hemisphere

I like art, in all it’s different
flavours. I also like to draw.

But more than that I like to use things that look nice. I mean, who doesn’t? I never lose sight of the original message and how it may be
interpreted by the end user. I hope that I can always look at something I’ve designed and say
‘I’d like to use that’.

Apart from my aptitude for typography and my awareness of space and colour, I like to doodle and use my imagination. That’s what has fuelled all my best designs. That and the deep down urge to be better than everyone else.

Overall, this side of my brain is obsessed with staying current with web trends and imagining
the future.

Right Hemisphere

I love solving puzzles. Logic puzzles, games, riddles and, more importantly, problems! After all, that is what our clients come to us for, amirite?

I like to spend a little extra time making sure my code is semantic, well structured and forward-thinking. I have found that working like this usually saves me alot of time further down the line.

I love technology in general. I often spend my personnal time cramming in as much tech knowledge as possible. I’m currently getting my head around node.js [link: http://nodejs.org].

To summarise, this side of my brain is posessed by finding out how everything works and staying up-to-date with the latest in web technology.

About Me

I'm Martin, I’m a long haired, 30 year-old senior agency creative. A passionate leader with a proclivity for translating complex ideas into slick, successful campaigns.

I’m a born and bred londoner who likes to design and build websites, apps and the like. A creative, yet logical thinker that’s easy to get along with. I graduated with a degree from the University of Arts London (Camberwell College).

I currently work at Creative Impact, a small agency in Twickenham that specialises in healthcare, where I have been for the last four years. Here, I am the Lead Designer, Lead Developer and Server Administrator. Amongst designing and coding, I also oversee a small team, manage suppliers, engage with clients and manage thier expectations.Working here has made me a versatile, agile worker that can slot in to any project, at any stage. As a result, I can meet short deadlines, work under pressure and it has led me to learn many assets of project deleopment. I also do a little freelancing in my spare time.

When I'm not coding or pushing pixels, you'll probably find me in the cinema or in the pub.

I specialise in website development, concept and identity. I create beautiful, usable, professional websites and apps using modern best practices.My code is semantic and seo friendly HTML and CSS, all of which are lovingly hand coded.

Key areas of experience include Website Development, App Development, Creative Direction, Art Direction and Senior-Level Design in Print, Digital, User Experience and Usability, Identity, Branding, Brand Management, Illustration, Animation and Audio Mixing. I have also been known to play guitar and keyboard from time to time.

Yeah, so, I pretty much do everything. Next, perhaps I should tackle making the web 3D!
